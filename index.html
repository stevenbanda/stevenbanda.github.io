Unseen
Opulence

"A 3D jewellery design collection exploring the theme of LUXURY REDEFINED — rendered with cinematic light and precision."

3D Rendering Rhino 3D Product Visualisation Jewellery
Featured
Project

My submission for a 3D jewelry design competition hosted by DebMarine — "Shining Lights" 2024. I placed top 6 in the entire country. Built from conception to execution in Rhino 3D.

The Renders

Each piece was modelled and rendered to capture light on precious metals and gemstones. Focus: macro-scale detail — craftsmanship only visible under extreme magnification.

Process

Sculpted with precision geometry, lit via three-point HDRI. Materials built from scratch — custom IOR values, subsurface scattering on pearls, physically accurate metal shaders.

Afrocode
Café

Afrocode Café merges African cultural motifs with a tech-forward aesthetic — geometric, structured, and rooted.

Logo System

The mark is built from a geometric icon inspired by traditional African architectural patterns, combined with a clean all-caps wordmark. Two variants — Color and Black.

Business Cards

Cards for the Owner — geometric brand pattern on the reverse. Front & back designed with full bleed pattern treatment.

Brand Extensions

Extended across touchpoints — coffee cup wraps, staff t-shirts, tote bags, aprons, and a full van livery.
