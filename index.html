003 — 3D Design
Unseen
Opulence

"A 3D jewellery visualisation series exploring the hidden beauty of luxury pieces — rendered with cinematic light and precision."

3D RenderingBlender / Cinema 4DProduct VisualisationJewellery
Featured
Project

Unseen Opulence reimagines fine jewellery as high-concept art objects. Each piece is rendered to reveal detail the naked eye often misses — facets, reflections, structure.

💍
💎
💎
The Renders

Each piece was modelled and rendered to capture light on precious metals and gemstones. Focus: macro-scale detail — craftsmanship only visible under extreme magnification.

🪙
💎
💍
Process

Sculpted with precision geometry, lit via three-point HDRI. Materials built from scratch — custom IOR values, subsurface scattering on pearls, physically accurate metal shaders.

Logo DesignBusiness CardsEmail SignatureBrand GuidelinesMerchandise
Afrocode
Café

Afrocode Café merges African cultural motifs with a tech-forward aesthetic — geometric, structured, and rooted. Located at #34 Independence Avenue, Windhoek, Namibia.

Logo System

The mark is built from a geometric icon inspired by traditional African architectural patterns, combined with a clean all-caps wordmark. Two colour variants — gold on white and gold on dark.
